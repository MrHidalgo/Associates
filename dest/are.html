TECHNOLOGY IS EVOLVING
AT BREAKNECK SPEED

By bringing together multidisciplinary groups of scientists, online, we help R&D companies keep pace with the latest breakthrough technologies emerging from the lab.

We do this by mapping technology landscapes, tracking how they develop in real-time, and forecasting long term trends/future innovation.

COLLECTIVE INTELLIGENCE

Almost all important problems are solved by groups of people rather than individuals alone. We believe that by working together in large teams we can deliver insight more intelligently, creatively and quickly than ever before possible.

Our vision is to redefine the way knowledge-work is done – investing in people and technology to develop, for the first time, truly effective crowdsourced research practices.

Work collaboratively with brilliant, likeminded experts

CASE STUDY: MAPPING

A market research and technology intelligence client required a detailed assessment of the impact of graphene and carbon nanotubes on next-generation battery technologies.

Through research and analysis of highly-cited, peer-reviewed journals, the team was able to provide deep technical specifications and add vital context relating to manufacturing processes, impurities, percentage loading and life cycles.

I was able to decide how much to contribute for each project depending on the workload of my PhD.

David Davidson

PHD Student, Bioengineering, Imperial College London
